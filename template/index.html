I'm a high school junior in California studying computer science in our engineering academy. I teach students to code and host hackathons at Cohack. I explore user interfaces and compiler theory with Moon.

Last summer, I interned as a junior engineer on the core team at Webflow, a company pushing the limits of no-code.

Over the past few years, I've been obsessed with open-source projects. Some highlights include Wade, a 1kb JavaScript text search library; Wing, a beautiful CSS library; and Slash, a small hash function.

Find my writing on my blog, my thoughts on Twitter, and my code on GitHub. Reach out by email.
